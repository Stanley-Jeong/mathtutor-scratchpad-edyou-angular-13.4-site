Providing a safe face for AI is our top priority.

  • Physical Safety
  • Emotional Wellbeing
  • Ongoing Human Oversight

We take a comprehensive approach to protecting the physical, emotional, and psychological wellbeing of every learner on our platform. With a combination of thoughtful AI design, rigorous content curation, and ongoing human oversight, we create secure learning environments where students can thrive academically while feeling cared for.

Physical Safety

All our Conversational AI Beings are governed by our Intelligent Curation Engine (ICE) which prevents inappropriate or harmful content from ever reaching learners. ICE blocks offensive language, flags concerning content for human review, and gives educators full visibility into knowledge sources.

We continually monitor emerging issues and upgrade our safeguards to block threatening or dangerous content. Learners can explore freely knowing edYOU has their back.

Emotional Wellbeing

Learning requires vulnerability so learners need emotional safety to thrive. Our AI Beings are designed to be compassionate, patient and supportive. They provide encouragement, never shaming struggling learners.

Learners shape the direction of conversations based on their comfort levels. Our AI has training to redirect any dialogue causing distress. We foster healthy self-esteem and growth mindset.

Ongoing Human Oversight

Ultimately, the human touch remains essential for safety. Our cross-functional team of child development experts, ethicists, educators, and technologists continuously monitor system interactions.

School administrators have real-time dashboard access to oversee all learner conversations. And students can reach a live support agent via in-app chat or email if needed.

By combining the scale of AI with human insight, edYOU creates learning experiences tailored to each student while keeping their wellbeing at the center. Because at edYOU, your children’s safety is our guiding light.
