Pay It Forward

edYOU was founded on the core idea of creating shared prosperity through education by empowering individuals with the tools to take charge of their learning. Our patented AI technology certainly stands at the leading edge of imagination, innovation, and impact. But more importantly, it exists to lift up humanity by making learning joyful, effective, and accessible to all.

That purpose led Dr. Michael Everest, our Founder, Chairman and CEO, to institute edYOU’s Pay It Forward pledge . This commitment states that for every institution subscribing to edYOU, we will provide our platform at no cost to an underserved school in the developing world. Knowledge should not be limited by geography or income.

In addition, for every 20 annual subscriptions in developed nations, our Pay It Forward pledge will grant a fully subsidized subscription to an underprivileged learner. We firmly believe that if one of us can dream, all of us can dream.

Our technology opens doors to the future. But our mission ensures those doors are open to everyone. Pay It Forward makes this win-win vision a reality. As an American company, we strive to uplift all people – especially the disadvantaged. Together, step by step, we will bring the light of learning to the world. Progress starts with paying it forward.
