Academic Integrity

Academic institutions need help to check on the integrity of their students’ work. We’re here to help schools and educational institutions maintain a fair and honest learning environment using the power of our platform.

edYOU’s guardrails are designed to ensure academic integrity in schools:
Integration: Our cutting-edge AI technology has a proprietary conversational platform that generates questions and answers. We also connect to LLM’s and a search engine to give students an integrated experience. Without the need to have multiple windows open on their computer or mobile phone makes learning more adventurous and robust.

Proctoring Solutions

Our platform gives real-time information as to the interaction the students have, time that is spent on the platform, what questions are asked, and answers given. This data is available to the teacher and institution, so when it needs to be checked, a student’s work comes with data to back the result. Test prep and exams are given on the platform and monitored as well. This prevents cheating behaviors such as looking up answers or using unauthorized resources during assessments and provides the educator with evidence based on the student’s work.

Data Informatics

The data gathered during the time spent on edYOU, the flipped classroom methodology of learning the lessons at home to get a head start in the classroom, helps teachers and students gather information. Educators and students can monitor their success and study interactions within our platform. edYOU assists stakeholders in adjusting their curriculum and study habits when necessary.

Behavioral Analysis

Our platform will have sentiment analysis and can also monitor students’ behavior, identifying unusual patterns of interaction or assessments. edYOU platform will identify speech patterns to help students in subject areas where the most help is needed.

Educational Modules

edYOU offers proprietary educational modules and tutorials to educate students and prepare for tests.

With edYOU’s commitment to academic integrity, educational institutions can foster a culture of academic honesty, discourage cheating, and ensure that students are equipped with the knowledge and tools to maintain their integrity throughout their academic journey. With robust data informatics, the student and teacher have data to help each other in their learning journey together and separately.
